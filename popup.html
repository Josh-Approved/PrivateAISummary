Private AI Summary uses Chrome's built-in Gemini Nano model, which requires:

  • Chrome 138 or newer
  • Windows 10/11 or macOS 13+
  • 4 GB+ GPU VRAM or 16 GB RAM
  • 22 GB free storage (for model)
  • Unmetered internet (first download only)

You may also need to enable the flag at chrome://flags/#optimization-guide-on-device-model and set it to "Enabled BypassPerfRequirement".

How it works
100% on-device

This extension uses Chrome's built-in Gemini Nano model, which runs entirely on your computer. When you click Summarize, your page content is processed locally — it never leaves your browser.

What that means for you
  • No account or sign-in required
  • Works fully offline after the first model download
  • Your content is never sent to any server
  • No usage data, analytics, or tracking of any kind
  • Free forever — no subscription, no paywall
How the model gets there

Chrome downloads Gemini Nano once in the background and stores it on your device. After that, all summarization happens locally using your CPU or GPU — completely private and offline.
