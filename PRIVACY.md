# Privacy Policy — Private AI Summary

Last updated: May 2, 2026

## Summary

Private AI Summary does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal data or browsing data of any kind.

## On-device processing

All summarization is performed entirely on your device using Chrome's built-in Gemini Nano model. Page content you summarize is never sent to any server — including ours. We have no servers.

## YouTube captions

When using the YouTube Summary feature, the extension fetches the video's caption file directly from YouTube's own servers (the same request YouTube's player makes). No caption data is sent to us.

## Local storage

The extension stores only two values locally on your device using chrome.storage.local: your last-selected output format and your last-selected language. These values never leave your device.

## No analytics or tracking

The extension contains no analytics code, no crash reporting, no usage tracking, and no third-party SDKs of any kind.

## No external assets

All fonts and visual assets are bundled within the extension. The extension makes no network requests of its own.
